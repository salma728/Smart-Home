========================================
Example Input Sequence for Smart Home System
========================================

This file shows a complete example of how to interact with the Smart Home System.

========================================
STEP-BY-STEP EXAMPLE:
========================================

1. Start the program:
   $ ./smarthome
   
   Output:
   === Smart Home System Started ===
   Type 'demo' to run automated demo, or use the menu for interactive control.

2. First, let's add some devices:
   
   Enter your choice: 1
   Enter light name: LivingRoomLight
   
   Enter your choice: 1
   Enter light name: BedroomLight
   
   Enter your choice: 2
   Enter thermostat name: HallThermostat
   
   Enter your choice: 3
   Enter camera name: FrontDoorCamera
   
   Enter your choice: 4
   Enter door lock name: MainDoorLock
   
   Enter your choice: 5
   Enter motion sensor name: LivingRoomSensor

3. Create groups:
   
   Enter your choice: 6
   Enter group name: LivingRoom
   
   Enter your choice: 6
   Enter group name: Entrance
   
   Enter your choice: 6
   Enter group name: Security

4. Add devices to groups:
   
   Enter your choice: 7
   Enter device name: LivingRoomLight
   Enter group name: LivingRoom
   
   Enter your choice: 7
   Enter device name: LivingRoomSensor
   Enter group name: LivingRoom
   
   Enter your choice: 7
   Enter device name: FrontDoorCamera
   Enter group name: Entrance
   
   Enter your choice: 7
   Enter device name: MainDoorLock
   Enter group name: Entrance
   
   Enter your choice: 7
   Enter device name: FrontDoorCamera
   Enter group name: Security
   
   Enter your choice: 7
   Enter device name: MainDoorLock
   Enter group name: Security
   
   Enter your choice: 7
   Enter device name: LivingRoomSensor
   Enter group name: Security

5. Control individual devices:
   
   Enter your choice: 8
   Enter device name: LivingRoomLight
   (Output: LivingRoomLight: turning ON)
   
   Enter your choice: 9
   Enter device name: LivingRoomLight
   (Output: LivingRoomLight: turning OFF)
   
   Enter your choice: 10
   Enter thermostat name: HallThermostat
   Enter temperature: 25
   (Output: HallThermostat: set temperature to 25)

6. Control groups:
   
   Enter your choice: 11
   Enter group name: LivingRoom
   (Output: Turns ON all devices in LivingRoom group)
   
   Enter your choice: 12
   Enter group name: Entrance
   (Output: Turns OFF all devices in Entrance group)

7. Set up automation (Observer Pattern):
   
   Enter your choice: 13
   Enter motion sensor name: LivingRoomSensor
   Enter device name to connect: LivingRoomLight
   (Output: [System] Connected LivingRoomSensor to LivingRoomLight)
   
   Enter your choice: 13
   Enter motion sensor name: LivingRoomSensor
   Enter device name to connect: FrontDoorCamera
   (Output: [System] Connected LivingRoomSensor to FrontDoorCamera)

8. Test motion detection:
   
   Enter your choice: 14
   Enter motion sensor name: LivingRoomSensor
   (Output: 
     LivingRoomSensor: motion detected
     [Observer] LivingRoomLight reacting to event: motion_detected
     LivingRoomLight: turning ON
     [Observer] FrontDoorCamera reacting to event: motion_detected
     FrontDoorCamera: recording started)

9. Set automation modes:
   
   Enter your choice: 15
   (Output: [System] Energy Saving Mode set)
   
   Enter your choice: 17
   (Output: [Automation] Applying Energy Saving Mode...)
   
   Enter your choice: 16
   (Output: [System] Comfort Mode set)
   
   Enter your choice: 17
   (Output: [Automation] Applying Comfort Mode...)

10. View group status:
    
    Enter your choice: 18
    Enter group name: LivingRoom
    (Output: 
      Group[LivingRoom]:
        - Light[LivingRoomLight] brightness=0 state=OFF
        - MotionSensor[LivingRoomSensor] observers=2)
    
    Enter your choice: 18
    Enter group name: Security
    (Output: Shows all security devices)

11. Test undo functionality:
    
    Enter your choice: 8
    Enter device name: BedroomLight
    (Output: BedroomLight: turning ON)
    
    Enter your choice: 19
    (Output: [System] Undo: Last command reversed
             BedroomLight: turning OFF)

12. Exit:
    
    Enter your choice: 0
    (Output: Exiting Smart Home System. Goodbye!)

========================================
QUICK DEMO MODE:
========================================

If you want to see all features automatically:

Enter your choice: demo
(or type "demo" when prompted)

This will run a complete automated demonstration of all features.

========================================
TIPS:
========================================

1. You can add multiple devices of the same type with different names
2. A device can belong to multiple groups
3. Motion sensors can trigger multiple devices
4. Use undo (option 19) to reverse the last command
5. Group operations affect all devices in that group
6. Type "demo" or choose option 20 to see an automated demonstration
